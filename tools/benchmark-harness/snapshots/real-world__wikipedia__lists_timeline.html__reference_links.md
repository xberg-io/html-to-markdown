**Timeline of computing** presents events in the history of computing
 organized by year and grouped into six topic areas: predictions and concepts,
 first use and inventions, hardware systems and processors, operating systems,
 programming languages, and new application areas.

**Detailed computing timelines**: [before 1950][58], [1950–1979][45], [1980–1989][46], [1990–1999][47], [2000–2009][48], [2010–2019][49], [2020–present][50]
